  • ABOUT US

    We are a gamer community that in the midst of so many ups and downs in game styles, we do not let go of the passion for motorsport. A community passionate and dedicated to what they do. A community that is divided between people forfun that only arcade, racing games to laugh and troll, oops. A group that takes it seriously, dedicates hours and hours in simulators looking for a perfect simulation, an incredible time and a lot of rivalry on the track. But in the end, they're all Limit Road, who end up getting into any game that has an old can, with an engine and four wheels for a good laugh.

  • about us

    Did you feel at home?! Follow the list of games and photos we took in our spare time. Enter our Discord, follow us on Social Networks and come be a Limit Road too.

    If the smell of gasoline is seductive to you, if the roar of the engine gives you goosebumps and if there are high doses of adrenaline coursing through your veins at over 100 beats per minute, well, my dear, we have a lot in common and there is a community where passion for cars is the most important rule, whether casual, or at the highest level of simracing, everyone is welcome here at Limit Road.

    Regardless of the game and style, if you have a car, we'll be there, but we make it clear that fun will always have its space, companionship will be our north and respect our guide along this long road of games, our main objective is simply having fun in the company of our friends, wandering from game to game, testing our rivals, trolling the distracted little friend and laughing at the noobiness of others, you can come too and become part of our family, and to not miss out on the news, follow us on our social networks.
